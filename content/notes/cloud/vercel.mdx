They coined the term immutable deployment and pioneered deploy previews. They
support static hosting but do not necessarily have the same optimizations in
place as specialized services like Gatsby Cloud for Gatsby. Vercel focuses on
Next.js hosting and serverless applications. They have fully embraced serverless
and discourage going old school.

## Findings

Vercel does not support symbolic links in Git repos:
